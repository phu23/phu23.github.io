MEET OUR BARISTA!

Our barista, S. Kuma, the owner of the Polar bear's cafe, is a true polar bear who was born in Hudson's Bay, Canada. According to Kuma, he was separated from his family when he was a child. Then, he got adopted by a kind and warm-hearted old couple, who taught him knowledge about coffee and coffee art. When Kuma turns 18, he decides to be independent, so he opens Polar bear's cafe, the first cafe that serves both humans and animals. He hopes his cafe can be so famous that he can reunite with his family someday.

P.S.: Kuma is a terrible puns maker although he never gets tired of making them.
