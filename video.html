OUR 2-week training is ideal for you if you are thinking of starting to work as cafe owners and professional baristas, or maybe just to be obsessed home coffee geeks! This course will offer the students plenty of practice with lots of coffee-decoration skills, and abundant background knowledge about coffee beans as well. After completion of this course, our students should fully understand blend composition how to choose the right beans for different coffee variables. They will also develop the skills to manipulate milk textures for various coffee decorations.
